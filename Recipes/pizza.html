Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a round, flat base of dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings like vegetables, meats, and herbs. It's baked in an oven until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted. Pizza comes in many styles, such as thin-crust, deep-dish, or Neapolitan, making it a versatile and beloved comfort food worldwide

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Yeast
  • Warm Water
  • Olive Oil
  • Sugar And Salt
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Pepper And Onions
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Veggies
  • Mushrooms,chicken,bacon
  • Parsley And Sausage

Steps

  1. Mix flour, yeast, water, olive oil, sugar, and salt to make the dough and let it rise.
  2. Roll out the dough into a round shape for the pizza base.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Add your favorite toppings like vegetables, meats, and herbs.
  6. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C) and bake the pizza for 10-15 minutes.
  7. Let the pizza cool for a few minutes, slice, and serve!
