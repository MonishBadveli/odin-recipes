Burger

Description

A burger is a popular fast food consisting of a ground meat patty (usually beef) placed between two buns, often accompanied by lettuce, tomato, cheese, pickles, and condiments like ketchup and mustard. Itâ€™s a customizable and easy-to-eat meal loved worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Ground beef or chicken (or vegetarian patty)
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Salt and peppe
  • Olive Oil or Butter

Steps

  1. Season the ground meat with salt and pepper, then form into burger patties.
  2. Heat olive oil or butter in a pan or grill and cook the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side, adding cheese in the last minute.
  3. Toast the burger buns lightly in the pan or oven.
  4. Assemble the burger by placing the cooked patty on the bottom bun, followed by lettuce, tomato, onions, and pickles.
  5. Add condiments like ketchup and mustard, then top with the other bun.
  6. Serve with fries or any sides you prefer.
