Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with rich fillings like meat sauce, cheese (such as ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan), and sometimes vegetables. The layers are baked in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, resulting in a hearty and flavorful casserole. It's a popular comfort food known for its satisfying texture and taste.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ground meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onions and garlic
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables
  • Herbs and spices

Steps

  1. Cook ground meat with onions, garlic, and tomato sauce to make the meat sauce.
  2. Mix ricotta cheese with Parmesan, salt, and pepper for the cheese filling.
  3. Spread meat sauce in a baking dish and layer with lasagna sheets, cheese, and more sauce.
  4. Repeat layers, ending with mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes, then uncover and bake for 10-15 more minutes.
  6. Let it rest for 10 minutes, slice, and serve.
